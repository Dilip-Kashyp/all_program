URL Encode this: <>20%20

- All numbers are written as they are
- Letters to change: 
  - <
  - >
  - %

 Accoding to ASCII character of (<)  is  60 . In hexadecimal,  is 
Written as 3C

Accoding to ASCII character of (>)  is   94. In hexadecimal,  is 
Written as 3E

Accoding to ASCII character of (%)  is 62  . In hexadecimal,  is 
Written as 25



The final URL Encoded string will look like:
%3C%3E%20%25%20
